Drop your personal files here:

  photo.jpg           — your profile photo (square works best, ~600x600 or larger)
  resume_en.pdf       — your CV in English
  resume_ru.pdf       — your CV in Russian
  resume_it.pdf       — your CV in Italian
  resume_fr.pdf       — your CV in French

If a file is missing, the site degrades gracefully:
  - missing photo.jpg shows your initials ("SF") in a styled circle
  - missing PDFs just produce a broken download link (the card still shows)
